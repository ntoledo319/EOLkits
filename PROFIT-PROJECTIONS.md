# EOLkits — Profit & Profit-Start Projections

_2026-06-22. Bottoms-up, deliberately conservative. These are projections with **wide uncertainty**,
not promises — the autopsy's first rule was "no fantasy numbers," and this honors it._

---

## Bottom line (read this first)

- **First dollar:** most likely **~6–10 weeks out (early-to-mid August 2026)**, base case. Could be
  ~3 weeks if an AI engine cites a `/fix` page early; could be never-in-2026 in the conservative case.
- **Sustained/meaningful revenue (>$1k/mo):** **Q4 2026 → Q1 2027**, anchored by the **Feb-2027
  Lambda runtime block wave** (the next real demand catalyst after AL2).
- **2026 total (base case):** **~$1,500–$5,000.** Conservative: ~$0–$500. Optimistic: ~$8k–$20k.
- **Margin ≈ 95%, so profit ≈ revenue.** Costs are ~$0 incremental (shared VPS, no ads, no LLM
  spend, free CLIs). **Break-even is literally sale #1** (~$299 covers years of marginal cost).
- **The hard truth:** the AL2 deadline (Jun 30, 8 days) will be **largely missed** — there is no time
  for a brand-new, zero-authority domain to rank, and the only autonomous channel (organic + AI-search)
  is slow. The real money is the Feb-2027 wave, *if* the corpus ranks by then.

---

## Why it's slow (the constraints that set the ceiling)

1. **Zero domain authority, zero backlinks, zero traffic history.** This is the single biggest
   limiter. Google rewards authority; a new site nobody links to can sit unseen for months even with
   great content. AI-search (ChatGPT/Perplexity, fed via IndexNow→Bing) is more merit/freshness-driven
   and is the better near-term bet — but it, too, weights authority.
2. **Organic + AI-search is the *only* channel.** Push channels are out: Reddit (policy-dead), HN/social
   (gated + new-account-banned), cold-email (would wreck the domain's email reputation that
   audit-delivery depends on). And no human capacity to do them. So no demand can be *pushed* — it can
   only be *captured* as it searches, which compounds slowly.
3. **Faceless brand, no social proof yet.** Suppresses conversion until the first reviews/stars/case
   studies exist. (Chicken-and-egg: needs sales to get proof to get sales.)
4. **Deadline timing.** AL2 (Jun 30) is too soon to capture. The next catalysts: **Node 20 Phase-3
   cliff (Sep 30, 2026)** — small bump — and the **Lambda Q1-2027 block cluster (Feb 1 / Mar 3, 2027)**
   for python3.8–3.10 and nodejs18/20 — the big one the corpus is built for.

## The model

surges to $399/$599 near a deadline) → upsell to **$1,499 Migration Pack** → **$19/mo Drift Watch**.

**Conversion (blended visitor → paid), honest for this situation:**
- Pure error-lookup traffic (`/fix`): **0.1–0.3%** (they mostly want the free fix).
- Deadline/guide/audit traffic: **0.5–2%** (real broken deploy + deadline + money-back guarantee).
- **Blended early: ~0.2–0.5%**, rising as proof accumulates.
- Early AOV ≈ **$299** (the Pack needs trust the brand hasn't earned yet; Drift adds slow MRR later).

**Traffic ramp** (≈25–30 sourced pages, IndexNow-submitted, on a zero-authority domain — the most
uncertain input; ranges are wide on purpose):

## What moves these numbers (sensitivities)

**Up:**
- **One human distribution push** (a single HN front-page, a newsletter mention, one strong backlink)
  would pull the entire curve **months earlier and several × higher** — it's the highest-ROI lever by
  far, and it's the one ruled out. If even 60 minutes of capacity ever appears, spend it here.
- **AI-search citation uptake** — if ChatGPT/Perplexity start citing the `/fix` corpus, that's the
  fastest realistic traffic and skews toward the optimistic column.
- **First sale → first testimonial/case study** breaks the proof chicken-and-egg and lifts conversion.

**Down (the conservative case is real):**
- New-domain SEO with zero backlinks can simply **never gain traction** — content alone often isn't
  enough. This is a genuine ~40%-likely outcome here, and it means **~$0**.
- AI engines may not cite a no-authority source.

## The honest framing

The machine is built, comprehensive, self-running, and pointed at real demand — at ~95% margin, so
**every dollar of revenue is essentially profit.** But on the **organic/AI-search-only** path with
**no promotion and no human capacity**, the realistic expectation is: **~$0 for the first 1–2 months,
a slow lumpy ramp through H2 2026, and the first genuinely meaningful month around the Feb-2027 Lambda
wave** — contingent on the pages actually ranking, which on a zero-backlink domain is the real risk.

This is the unavoidable arithmetic of "no distribution." The product economics are excellent; the
*acquisition* is the constraint. The fastest way to beat these projections by an order of magnitude
remains a single act of human distribution — everything autonomous to maximize the organic path is
already done and running.
